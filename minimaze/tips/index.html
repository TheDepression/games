Basic Movement & Controls

Master Frog uses simple directional controls to navigate through the maze. Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your frog character around obstacles and enemies.

Watch out for dangerous enemies like the shark shown above! They can end your journey quickly if you're not careful.

Pro Tip: Take your time to observe enemy movement patterns before making your move.

Power-ups & Items

Throughout your adventure, you'll find various items that can help you on your journey. Keys unlock doors, boats help you cross water, and hammers break through walls.

Always check your inventory to see what tools you have available. Some puzzles require specific items to solve!

Some enemies like snakes guard valuable items. Plan your approach carefully to avoid unnecessary deaths.

Remember: Every death counts toward your final score, so use items wisely!

Enemy Strategies

Each enemy type has unique behavior patterns. Learning these patterns is key to avoiding deaths and achieving a better score.

Some enemies move in predictable patterns, while others react to your presence. Observe carefully before approaching!

Strategy Tip: Sometimes the best approach is to wait and let enemies move away from your intended path.

Remember: your goal is to minimize deaths, not to defeat enemies. Avoidance is often better than confrontation.

Advanced Techniques

For experienced players looking to achieve the best scores, timing and route optimization are crucial.

Learn the most efficient paths through each level. Sometimes a longer route with fewer enemy encounters is faster overall.

Speed Run Tips:

  • Memorize enemy spawn locations
  • Plan your inventory usage in advance
  • Practice consistent movement patterns
  • Know when to take calculated risks

The leaderboard rewards both low death counts and fast completion times. Find the balance that works for your playstyle!
